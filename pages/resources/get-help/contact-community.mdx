# Contact & Community

Thanks for checking out Replay! We want these docs to be as helpful as possible, so let us know how we can improve. 

## Contact

If you have any feedback or run into a technical issue with Replay, you can reach us directly at:

- [Discord](https://replay.io/discord)
- [GitHub](https://github.com/replayio/devtools/issues/new/choose)
- [support@replay.io](mailto:support@replay.io)

## Join our Community

We love building and learning alongside our community. If you'd like to join, feel free to hop in to our [Discord](https://replay.io/discord/yTmrss5xfR) server and say hello in #introductions!

### Channels!

- **#introductions** Just joined our server? Tell us a bit about what you do, where you're from, or anything else that you'd like to share.
- **#general** This is where discussions about Replay happen and you can interact with the community.
- #**bug-reports** Discovered an issue? Feel free to share it here and we'll take a look.
- **#development** We build out in the open. In fact, our UI is open sourced and we'd love your help.
- **#design** We design out in the open and would love your thoughts. Also feel free to follow along with our [design journal](https://www.notion.so/Design-Journal-d5cfda43a5b04fbe876452ab4e832b92?pvs=21).

### Code of Conduct:

Our Discord is all about learning with and giving back to the community! To facilitate this, it is pivotal for us to create and maintain an inclusive space so that everyone can learn and grow together.

Please read our [Code of Conduct](https://github.com/replayio/devtools/blob/master/code-of-conduct.md) thoroughly and adhere to it. Any violations of the Code of Conduct will be followed up with a warning and continued violations will further lead to banishment from the server.
